# BubbleFish Nexus — Corporate Contributor License Agreement

This Corporate Contributor License Agreement ("Corporate CLA") establishes the terms under which an organization and its employees contribute to BubbleFish Nexus.

**Sign this if you are contributing on behalf of your employer or another organization.** Individual contributors who are not acting on behalf of an organization should sign the [Individual CLA](CLA.md) instead.

---

## Why a Corporate CLA?

Many employers have intellectual property policies that give them rights over work their employees produce, even outside of work hours in some jurisdictions. The Corporate CLA ensures that:

1. The organization acknowledges and authorizes contributions from its employees
2. The organization grants the necessary licenses for those contributions
3. Individual employees don't need to determine on their own whether their employer's IP policy applies

Once an organization signs the Corporate CLA, all authorized employees and contractors can contribute without signing the Individual CLA separately.

**"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship, including modifications or additions to existing work, that an Authorized Contributor intentionally submits to the Project for inclusion. This includes code, documentation, configuration, tests, and any other material submitted via pull request, issue, email, or any other method of communication with the project maintainers.

**"Authorized Contributor"** means any employee, contractor, or agent of Your organization who is authorized by You to make Contributions on Your behalf. You are responsible for maintaining a list of Authorized Contributors and providing it to BubbleFish Technologies, Inc. upon request.

**"Project"** means BubbleFish Nexus and any associated repositories maintained under the `bubblefish-tech` GitHub organization.

(a) You are legally entitled to grant the above licenses on behalf of Your organization and Your Authorized Contributors.

### 6. Authorized Contributor management

You agree to:

(a) Maintain a current list of Authorized Contributors (by GitHub username or email).

(b) Notify BubbleFish Technologies, Inc. when individuals are added or removed from the authorized list.

(c) Ensure that Authorized Contributors are aware of and comply with the terms of this Corporate CLA.

### 9. Notification of third-party content

### 10. Future changes

BubbleFish Technologies, Inc. may update this Corporate CLA for future contributions. Any such update will be posted in the project repository. Updates apply only to Contributions made after the update date. Contributions made under a prior version of this Corporate CLA remain governed by that version.

## How to sign

Send an email to **contribute@bubblefish.sh** from an authoritative email address within your organization (e.g., legal@, engineering@, cto@) with:

- Your organization's legal name
- The name and title of the person signing on behalf of the organization
- A list of GitHub usernames authorized to contribute under this Corporate CLA
- The statement: "On behalf of [Organization Name], I have read and agree to the BubbleFish Nexus Corporate Contributor License Agreement."

We will confirm receipt and add the authorized usernames to the CLA bot's allowlist within two business days.

To add or remove authorized contributors after signing, send an update email to the same address.

---

## Relationship to the Individual CLA

Once an organization signs the Corporate CLA, its Authorized Contributors do not need to sign the Individual CLA separately. If an Authorized Contributor has already signed the Individual CLA, the Corporate CLA supersedes it for contributions made on behalf of the organization.

Contributions made by an individual outside the scope of their employment (e.g., personal projects, contributions made on their own time that are not covered by the organization's IP policy) are governed by the Individual CLA, not the Corporate CLA.
