# BubbleFish Nexus — Contributor License Agreement

Thank you for your interest in contributing to BubbleFish Nexus. This Contributor License Agreement ("CLA") establishes the terms under which contributions are accepted into the project.

**You must sign this CLA before your first contribution can be merged.** A CLA bot will comment on your first pull request with a link to sign electronically. It takes about two minutes.

---

## Why a CLA?

BubbleFish Nexus is dual-licensed:

- **AGPL-3.0** for community use (free, open source, copyleft)
- **Commercial license** for organizations that need it (sold by BubbleFish Technologies, Inc.)

For the commercial license to legally cover contributions from the community, BubbleFish Technologies, Inc. needs a license grant from each contributor. That is what this CLA provides.

**What this means for you:**

- You keep copyright ownership of everything you write. The CLA does not assign your copyright away.
- Your contributions are always available under AGPL-3.0. That never changes.
- BubbleFish Technologies, Inc. can additionally distribute your contributions under the commercial license. This is what lets the project sustain itself financially.
- You can use your own contributions in any other project, for any purpose, at any time. The CLA does not restrict your rights.

This is the same model used by Grafana Labs, MongoDB (pre-SSPL), Neo4j, and many other dual-licensed open-source projects.

## How to sign

The CLA bot will automatically comment on your first pull request with a signature link. Follow the link and sign electronically. The bot tracks your signature and will not ask again on subsequent pull requests.

If you prefer to sign manually, send an email to **contribute@bubblefish.sh** with:

- Your full legal name
- Your GitHub username
- The statement: "I have read and agree to the BubbleFish Nexus Contributor License Agreement."

## Corporate contributions

If you are contributing on behalf of your employer or another organization, the organization must sign the [Corporate CLA](CLA-CORPORATE.md) instead of (or in addition to) this individual CLA. The Corporate CLA covers all employees and contractors of the signing organization.
