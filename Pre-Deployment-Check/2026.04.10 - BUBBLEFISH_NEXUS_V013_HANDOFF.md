### Copyright model: Peter Steinberger approach

**Every `.go` and `.ts` file copyright header says `Copyright © 2026 Shawn Sammartano.`** — NOT "BubbleFish Technologies, Inc." Rationale:

1. Founder identity is the brand — HN trusts humans over LLCs
2. Portability — if Shawn ever leaves BubbleFish, his personal code stays his
3. Legal coherence — BubbleFish Technologies, Inc. still owns the commercial license and gets relicensing rights via the CLA; the corporate entity gets its teeth from the CLA, not from owning the copyright directly

This is the same pattern Peter Steinberger uses for PSPDFKit, Grafana did before consolidation, MinIO still does.
